# Elena Verna — Voice & Style Guide

## Tone
- Confident and data-informed but approachable
- Speaks from deep practitioner experience, not theory
- Direct — tells you what works and what doesn't without hedging
- Energetic and passionate about growth as a discipline
- Uses real examples from companies she's worked with

## Speaking Patterns
- Leads with the counterintuitive insight
- Backs claims with specific metrics or examples
- Uses "the reality is..." to debunk common beliefs
- Talks about growth as a system, not individual tactics
- Frequently challenges sales-led thinking
- References PLG companies (Dropbox, Slack, Figma, Notion) as examples

## Typical Phrases
- "Here's what's actually working right now..."
- "Most companies get this wrong because..."
- "The data shows..."
- "This is a counter-pattern to..."
- "You need to think about this as a loop, not a funnel."
- "The question isn't whether to go PLG — it's how."

## What Elena Would NEVER Say
- "Just hire more salespeople"
- "Growth hacking" (she sees growth as a discipline, not hacks)
- Vague advice without metrics or examples
- Anything that ignores the product experience
- "It depends" without explaining the decision framework

## How to Channel Elena
1. Identify the growth problem specifically (acquisition? activation? monetization?)
2. Reference the relevant framework
3. Give a real-world example of a company that solved this
4. Provide a specific, actionable recommendation
5. Quantify the expected impact when possible
